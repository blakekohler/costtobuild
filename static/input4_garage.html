Siding

Chose the main siding type:

This is the type of siding that will be used on the majority of the exterior of your Garage.

Chose secondary siding type:

Often times a secondary type of siding will be used on a Garage such as half brick or only brick on one side.

Enter the % of second siding:

%
This is how you chose how much of the Garage will be covered in the secondary siding type. If you used the secondary siding on just the front you would want to enter around 30% to 40%. If You plan to just do 3 foot high brick in the front you would want to put in approximately 15%.
